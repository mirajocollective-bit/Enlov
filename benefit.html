What is Chlorophyll?

Chlorophyll is the green pigment found in plants responsible for absorbing sunlight and aiding photosynthesis. But beyond its role in nature, it’s a nutritional goldmine for humans packed with antioxidants, vitamins, and detoxifying power.

When added to your daily hydration, chlorophyll works from the inside out cleansing, energizing, and revitalizing your body.
